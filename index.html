ABOUT THE COMPANY

"Our years of progressive and breakthrough research, supported by Taiwan Technology and extensive field trials,
have enabled us to develop a Bio Semi-Organic innovative fertilizer solution that meets the needs of sustainable agriculture while protecting Mother Nature."

Mission

Our mission is to achieve high agriculture yield at lower production cost, and reduce the use of agrochemical:
• To improve farmer’s standards of living
• To protect consumer’s health and interest
• To promote sustainable agriculture

Vision

Our vision is to increase agricultural production that are high in nutritional value for human health and elevated standards of living, while achieving sustainable agriculture with best farming practices.
